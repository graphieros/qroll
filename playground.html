You are in control

Qroll exposes its interface to allow you to add actions to scroll anywhere you want.

qroll.slideDown()
qroll.slideUp()
qroll.slideToIndex(1)

UI components

Qroll handles dialog elements for you. You just have to provide an id, a class, and optional data attributes. Place your dialog div at the same level as your slides, and we'll do the rest.


<div id="qroll-parent" class="qroll-main qroll-loop qroll-tooltip">
    <div><h1> Slide 1 </h1></div>
    <div>
      <h1> Slide 2 </h1>
      <button onclick="qroll.openDialog('myId')"> Say hi </button>
    </div>

    <div id="myId" class="qroll-dialog" data-title="My Title" data-css-classes="my-dialog-class-1 my-dialog-class-2">
      Hello, World!
    </div>
</div>
